EUSEBIO HIGH SCHOOL LMS

Eusebio High School formerly known as Eusebio High School Rosario Annex has grown from a small high school situated at Maybunga Elementary School to a splurging annex into an independent large high school. At present Dr. Gilbert O. Inocencio leads and manages the 5, 441 leaners, 182 teaching personnel, and 33 non-teaching personnel of Eusebio High School.
