SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL STRANDS IN EHS

STEM

This strand focuses on advanced concepts in science, math, engineering, and technology. It prepares students for college courses like medicine, engineering, architecture, and computer science. It develops analytical thinking, problem-solving, and innovation skills.

ABM

ABM is designed for students who are interested in business, entrepreneurship, economics, and management. It teaches skills in leadership, financial literacy, marketing, and business planning, ideal for future entrepreneurs, accountants, and business professionals.

GAS

GAS offers a broad and flexible curriculum for students who are undecided about their future course. It combines subjects from the STEM, ABM, and HUMSS strands, making it a versatile option for various college programs. It enhances communication, critical thinking, and decision-making skills.

HUMSS

HUMSS is for students interested in people, culture, society, and communication. It focuses on subjects like philosophy, literature, history, and political science. It is perfect for those planning to take up education, journalism, psychology, law, or social work in college.
